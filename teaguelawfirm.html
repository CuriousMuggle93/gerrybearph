Teague Law Firm

  • Homepage
  • Portfolio
  • Project

For this project, I designed and developed a modern website for Teague Law Firm, a legal practice focused on providing professional legal services. The goal was to create a high-credibility online presence while also implementing a strong SEO foundation to help the firm attract potential clients through organic search.

The website was built using WordPress and Elementor Pro, with a focus on performance, accessibility, and search visibility.

Client Challenge

Before the project, the firm needed:.

A modern, professional website

  • A structure that clearly explains their legal services
  • Improved search engine visibility
  • A site that builds trust and authority with potential clients

Law firms operate in a highly competitive SEO environment, so the website needed to be designed not only for aesthetics but also for search performance and lead generation.

My Strategy

To achieve these goals, I approached the project with a combination of UX design, SEO optimization, and content strategy.

The strategy included:

  • SEO-optimized website structure
  • Keyword research for legal services
  • On-page SEO implementation
  • Mobile-first responsive design
  • Technical performance optimization

Website Design & Development

I designed and developed the entire website from the ground up using WordPress and Elementor Pro.

Key pages included:

  • Homepage
  • About the Firm
  • Practice Areas/li>
  • Contact Page

The layout was designed to guide visitors toward key actions such as contacting the firm or learning about legal services.

The visual identity of the site was also developed as part of the project. This included:

  • Logo design
  • Custom banners and graphics
  • Color palette and typography
  • Professional imagery aligned with the legal industry

These design elements help communicate credibility, trust, and professionalism, which are essential for law firm websites.

SEO Strategy & Keyword Research

A core part of this project involved implementing search engine optimization (SEO) to help the firm attract potential clients online.

I conducted keyword research to identify relevant legal search terms that potential clients might use when searching for legal assistance.

Examples of targeted keyword categories included:

  • legal services near me
  • law firm consultation
  • legal representation services
  • attorney services

These keywords were integrated naturally throughout the website’s content, headings, and metadata to improve search engine visibility.

On-Page SEO Implementation

To improve search rankings, I implemented several on-page SEO best practices, including:

  • SEO-optimized page titles
  • Meta descriptions
  • Structured headings (H1, H2, H3)
  • Keyword-optimized service descriptions
  • Internal linking between pages
  • Image optimization and alt text

These keywords were integrated naturally throughout the website’s content, headings, and metadata to improve search engine visibility.

Technical SEO & Performance Optimization

In addition to content optimization, I focused on improving the technical performance of the website.

Key improvements included:

  • Mobile-responsive design
  • Fast page loading speed
  • Clean site structure
  • SEO plugin integration (RankMath)
  • Optimized images and media

These technical improvements ensure the site performs well across devices and meets modern SEO standards.

User Experience & Conversion Design

Since law firm websites are focused on client inquiries, I designed the site to guide visitors toward contacting the firm.

This included:

  • Clear calls-to-action
  • Easy-to-navigate menu structure
  • Strategically placed contact forms
  • Trust-building visual elements

These UX decisions help turn website visitors into potential leads.

Tools & Technologies Used

  • WordPress
  • Elementor Pro
  • Yoast SEO / RankMath
  • Google Analytics
  • Google Search Console
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Canva

These UX decisions help turn website visitors into potential leads.

Project Outcome

The final result was a modern, professional law firm website that successfully combines design, usability, and SEO.

Key outcomes included:

  • A strong professional online presence
  • Improved search engine readiness
  • A fully responsive website optimized for all devices
  • A scalable platform that can support future content and SEO growth

The client was very pleased with the result, highlighting the website’s clean design, improved functionality, and search-optimized structure.
